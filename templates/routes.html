The Nature Lover's Trail

Experience the breathtaking natural beauty of Mangatarem. This route takes you through protected landscapes, waterfalls, and scenic mountain roads.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Start at Manleluag Spring Protected Landscape for a morning dip.
  • Trek to Timmanguyob Falls for a refreshing adventure.
  • Drive through Daang Kalikasan for sunset views.

Historical Heritage Walk

Step back in time and explore the rich history of Mangatarem through its colonial architecture and landmarks.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Visit St. Raymund de Penafort Church.
  • Explore the Old Municipal Hall.
  • Walk through the Ancestral Houses in the town center.
