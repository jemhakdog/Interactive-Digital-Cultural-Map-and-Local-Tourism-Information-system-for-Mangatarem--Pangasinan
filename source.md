IT Capstone Project Title Suggestion #2:

Interactive Digital Cultural Map and Local Tourism Information System

Description:

The Interactive Digital Cultural Map and Local Tourism Information System is a web-based platform designed to showcase the city’s cultural identity, community highlights, and tourism offerings across its barangays. The system provides an interactive map that allows users to explore local cultural spots, traditions, attractions, events, and significant community features. By consolidating cultural and tourism-related information into one accessible digital resource, the system aims to promote local pride, support educational use, and enhance tourism visibility for residents, students, and visitors.

Suggested Key Features:

1. Interactive Cultural Map
Clickable map of the City/Municipality
Barangay-level cultural highlights
Photos, descriptions, and media per location
Zoom and navigation tools

2. Cultural and Tourism Information Portal
Profiles of each barangay’s cultural assets, traditions, local practices, and points of interest
Listings of local tourism spots, eateries, attractions, and unique community features

3. Events and Festival Directory
Calendar of local festivities
Details on barangay events and citywide celebrations
Announcements of upcoming cultural activities

4. Multimedia Gallery
Photo and video collections of cultural events
Uploadable media showcasing local traditions and community activities

5. Search and Filter Tools
Search by barangay, cultural category, attraction type, or event
Quick access to specific cultural or tourism-related information

6. Admin and Contributor Module
Authorized barangay representatives can add or update cultural information
Admin approval workflow for content management
Easy content updates for events and photos

7. Tourist Guide and Suggested Routes
Recommended routes for exploring cultural and local tourism spots
Itinerary suggestions based on themes (e.g., food tour, historical walk, community landmarks)

8. Analytics Dashboard (Optional but strong for capstone)
Insights on most-viewed locations and attractions
Trends on user engagement and cultural content access
